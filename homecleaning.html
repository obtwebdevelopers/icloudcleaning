Home Cleaning

To busy to clean your own house? Got kids who just don't know how to clean up after themselves? Have no fear Icloud Cleaning Services can take care of you messes for you. Got some dusty surfaces to clean? We can handle that for you. We can also clean those dishes you don't want to clean. After that is all done we'll clean your entire kitchen. We can also handle your bathroom. From the toilet to the shower, we can make your bathroom look like is never been used. We'll also handle all bedrooms and living rooms. We can dust the room, make the bed and change your linens. We will also be sweeping and mopping the living room and dusting all the furniture within it. At the end of our services your house will be left looking just like new. We also give extra services upon request. These services include: inside fridge cleaning, inside oven cleaning, inside cabinets cleaning, interior window cleaning, and laundry wash and dry.

***Note that we do not offer the following services. We do not offer Deep stain removal, Mold removal, Infestation, Insect removal, and Exterior Window Cleaning.***
