Business Cleaning

Here at Icloud Cleaning Services we take care all of your office cleaning needs. We handle everything from lobbies to restrooms. Got an elevator that needs cleaning we've got your solution. Our group also takes care of any trash related needs of your business. From emptying waste baskets to transporting trash to that smelly dumpster that no one wants to be around. We also take care of small scale maintenance. This includes sweeping scrubbing floors, and dusting furniture and equipment. It also includes replacing light bulbs, Washing windows, and handling repair requests. We also prepare your office for meetings so that you don't have to. With Icloud Cleaning Services, your work area will not only be up to standard, it will be so clean you won't want to hire another cleaning service! We also offer extra services upon request including: Cleaning interior and exterior front doors, vacuuming all carpeted areas, sweeping and mopping hard surfaces, and vacuuming all hard surfaces.

***Note that we do not offer the following services. We do not offer Deep stain removal, Mold removal, Infestation, Insect removal, and Exterior Window Cleaning.***
